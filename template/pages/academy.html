DTT Academy

The company believes that the future of the business will be determined by the caliber of the people it employs and how well the employees are trained.

In determining its recruitment plans the company launched the independent academy. The business will continue to look for driven and motivated individuals who are passionate about investments. Due to the fact that financial markets are still a debatable issue in Tanzania, the company decided to take an action in creating the first academy that will nurture novice traders until they reach a professional level. The company aims to add more value in Tanzania mainly to Undergraduates and the like who tend to lack for potential job hiring after completion studies but face a competitive job market in the current economy. The company does not limit any other party that is determined, willing, motivated and passionate individuals that would love to pursue a career on investing into financial markets.
